Smart Tips to Reduce Food Waste

Learn practical ways to minimize food waste at home!

Proper Storage

Store fruits and vegetables in the correct way to extend their shelf life.

Meal Planning

Plan your meals for the week to avoid over-purchasing.

Creative Leftovers

Use leftovers in new recipes to avoid waste.

Understand Expiration Dates

Know the difference between sell by, best before, and use by dates.

Buy in Bulk Wisely

Only buy in bulk if you can use the items before they go bad.

Check Your Pantry Regularly

Regularly check for items nearing their expiration date.

Portion Control

Serve smaller portions to avoid uneaten food on plates.

Compost Food Scraps

Composting can turn scraps into nutrient-rich soil.

Repurpose Food Scraps

Use vegetable scraps to make broth or smoothies from overripe fruit.

Donate Unused Food

Consider donating excess non-perishable food items.

Educate Yourself on Food Preservation

Learn techniques for preserving food, like pickling or canning.

Cook with Friends or Family

Organize cooking days to use up ingredients and share meals.
